Competition day tips
01
Use Scratch offline. The test uses Scratch 3.29 desktop — not the website. Practice with the app so nothing looks unfamiliar.
02
Algorithm first. 20 of 30 Section C points come from a correct algorithm and working program. Get those before decorating.
03
Label your arrows. Every arrow out of a decision diamond must say Yes or No. Missing labels lose precision points.
04
Check edge cases. What happens at exactly 0? Exactly 100? Judges check the boundaries — make sure your if/else covers them.
